About the Organization

Welcome to VolunTeen, a passionate group students who believe in the power of youth to make a difference. Our journey began with a shared desire to contribute to our communities and create positive change. Fueled by our own experiences as young volunteers, we recognized the need for a platform that connects middle and high school students with volunteer opportunities aligned with their interests.
